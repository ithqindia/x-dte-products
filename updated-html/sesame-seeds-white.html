Description

Natural Sesame seeds are considered as the most nutritious seeds because of presence of proteins, iron, calcium and phosphorus and other important minerals. Hulled Sesame seeds are relatively saltier and delicious as compared to natural seeds. Our Natural sesame seeds are produced and re-cleaned in such a way that its intrinsic richness, nutrition and flavor remain intact. These uniformly bold size seeds have nutty, slightly sweet flavor and are usually bright white in color. These are much softer and easily digestible, that retain all the characteristics of natural seeds. Natural sesame seeds are extensively used in breads, burgers, sauces, colors, crackers, spreads, drinks, granola, candies, soups and various other bakery and confectionery products to enrich its taste and nutritional values.
