Papad mung dal plain is a thin Indian wafer described as a cracker or flat bread, circular in shape. It is served as an accompaniment with an Indian meal or eaten as an appetizer or a snack and can be eaten with toppings. Our organic Papad Moong Plain is made by using high quality moong (green gram dal). Aroma and flavor of these mung dal Papad are pleasing and make them ideal for serving in festive parties. Use of cumin seeds enhances its taste.
