Description

Garam meaning “hot” and Masala symbolizing “Mixture”. Known for generating heat in the body, used by traders as they moved from one place to another, to ward off the cold and other related conditions, to keep them moving. Specially made with chosen Organic ingredients. This is a mixture of ground aromatic spices and heart of the most Indian Dishes. The word “Garam” is not heat, it refers to spice intensity.
